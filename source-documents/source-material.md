## 4. Shopping Cart Functions

- View products in the shopping cart.
- Increase product quantity.
- Decrease product quantity.
- Prevent quantity from going below 1.
- Calculate item total.
- Calculate cart subtotal.
- Add an order note with a maximum of 256 characters.
- Remove one product.
- Remove all products with confirmation.
- Display an empty-cart state.
- Continue to the checkout information step.
